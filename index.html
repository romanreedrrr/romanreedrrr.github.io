Views by Drake

Drake created this album based of the Summer and Winter of Toronto

"Views" by Drake, dropped in 2016, marks a big moment for the Canadian rapper. It's like a window into his thoughts. He's talking about fame, love, and figuring himself out. You've got these deep tracks like "Redemption" and "Keep the Family Close," but also the bangers like "One Dance" and "Controlla" that get you moving. The title is a nod to his hometown, Toronto, and it feels like he's showing you around the city. Overall, "Views" is just smooth vibes and catchy tunes, making it a standout in Drake's music lineup.
